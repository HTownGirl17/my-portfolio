Who I am

Designer & developer based out of Houston, TX

I am an experienced communication professional who spent 13 1/2 years as a Radio Producer. I helped my co-workers with their technical problems and now I want to help others, too. I have spent my life working on computers, both Mac and PC computers. I got my start on creating websites when I was in high school. Before Facebook was around, I built my first website to show off images of things that I had done to my friends and family that lived far away. This lead me to a part-time job at an advertisting agency assisting in creating websites for businesses. Although I went a different direction after getting my bachelor's degree, website designing and developing was never far from my mind. I created a call manager for the radio station I worked for and a site for a doctor who hosts a talk show on the radio station.

Now after completing a web development bootcamp and a React course on Udemy and soon to be graduating with an Associate of Applied Science Degree in Mobile/Web Application Development, I want to use the knowledge I have learned to create and manage websites for your business. I am a team player, organized, customer service oriented and a master at multitasking. My passion is for creativity. I find it exciting to explore new ways to create interactivity with the user. Learning new things is how we grow and evolve as people. I am dedicated to learning more and more about design and development which is why I am going for my master's degree in Computer Science. I hope my dedication will inspire you to hire for your next project.
